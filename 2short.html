Life's 2 Short Works a Lot


Life's 2 Short Works a Lot is a 2 player puzzle-platformer that plays with the use of one's life to enable the progression of the game. Each level requires each player to die in specific ways in order to set up the success of the other. Player deaths spawn platforms that power up whoever is brave enough to step over their graves.

2 Short (as I've chosen to abbreviate) was an exercise in 2 dimensional movement systems, sprite animation, and level design. I absolutely loved figuring out how to combine movement systems that felt rewarding and challenging with puzzle design. This is a small-scaled project, spanning over only 9 levels that might be revisited in the future.
