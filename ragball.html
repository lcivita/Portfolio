Ragball

Ragball is exactly what you think it is. It is a soccer game where every single aspect of player movement is controlled with in-game physics. By blending the complete 'jankyness' of physics based controllers with a bit of invisible aimbot, this turned out to be my game which received the most positive feedback, despite the core of it being coded in the span of less than a week. Ragdolls are awesome.
