GLSL was my starting point in shader programming. My initial experience was short, but extremely informative. With the parallel processing GPU principles I acquired from these studies, I was able to implement shaders into my game development pipeline.

In order to showcase my GLSL programming, I have decided to make all of the backgrounds in my portfolio be entirely composed of shaders I wrote for WEBGL. I am not entirely sure how far I will have gotten at the point of your viewing, but hopefully enough pages will be filled with eye-candy to satisfy your every need!

vec2 pos;
uniform float time;
uniform vec2 resolution;
